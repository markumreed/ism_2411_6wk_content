Chart Selection
Bar = categories. Line = time trend. Histogram = continuous distribution. Know for quiz and Capstone rubric.
plt.bar() vs sns.barplot()
plt.bar() shows exact values you pass in. sns.barplot() computes mean automatically and adds confidence interval error bars.
